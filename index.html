Introduction

In the Spring semester of 2022, UNA rolled out with a new WIFI program called Apogee.

The idea when partnering with Apogee was to give each student that lives on campus control over their WIFI devices and to limit each resident to 7 devices.

Apogee has companied with partnered with many schools over the US such as East Tennessee State University, Geroge Mason University, Georgia Southern,UAB, University of Kansas, and many more - thus making the company appear credible.

Unfortunately, after adopting the program and it being used by UNA residents, many problems were identified.
